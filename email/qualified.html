How to adopt voters and send letters

  1. Sign in at https://votefwd.org
  2. Click the “Adopt 5 Voters” button.
  3. Click the "Download all" button.
  4. Print the PDF that's downloaded to your computer. Notice the return address and the recipients' addresses on the first page.
  5. On each letter hand-write:
    1. The salutation — e.g. "Dear Katherine". (Notice that the recipient's name and address are also on each letter at the bottom in small type for easy reference).
    2. Your completion of the sentence "I have pledged to vote in every election because..."
    3. Your signature at the bottom.
  6. (optional) Take and post a photo of the top half of the letter social media. Use the tag #VoteForward or @votefwd, and say something about why you're volunteering to send letters. (Make sure the "Pledge Code" on the letter isn't visible in your photo!)
  7. Hand-write the recipient's address on an ordinary envelope
  8. Hand-write your first name and last initial and the return address on the envelope.
  9. Fold and stuff the letter in the envelope.
  10. Put a stamp on it. Stamps with personality are encouraged! Mr. Rogers, for example.
  11. Set aside until October. We will all send our letters one week before the election, on October 30.
  12. Click the green checkboxes on the website to mark letters "prepped" so we know it's ready to go.
  13. Repeat for all your adoptees...then adopt some more. :)
