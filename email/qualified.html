Vote Forward Instructions

  1. Sign in at https://votefwd.org
  2. Click the “Adopt 5 Voters” button.
  3. Click the "Download all" button.
  4. Print the PDF. Notice the addresses on page 1.
  5. On each letter hand-write:
    1. The salutation — e.g. "Dear Mr./Ms. X". (Note: the recipient's info is on each letter at the bottom in small type for easy reference).
    2. The completion of the sentence "I have pledged to vote in every election because..."
    3. Your signature at the bottom.
  6. Hand-write the recipient's name and address on an ordinary envelope
  7. Hand-write your first name and last initial and the return address on the envelope.
  8. Fold and stuff the letter in the envelope.
  9. Put a stamp on it. Stamps with personality are encouraged! Mr. Rogers, for example.
  10. Set aside until late October. We will all send our letters one week before the election, on October 30 (we will remind you!).
  11. Click the green checkboxes on the website to mark each letter "prepped" so we know it's ready to go.
  12. Repeat for all your adoptees...then adopt some more. :)
